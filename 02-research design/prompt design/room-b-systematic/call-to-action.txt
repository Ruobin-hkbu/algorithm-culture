Design ethical call-to-action elements that:
- Encourage action without using pressure tactics or manipulation
- Use clear, direct language that respects user autonomy
- Respect consumer choice and decision-making process
- Provide transparent information about next steps
- Consider accessibility in phrasing and design
- Avoid exploiting urgency unless genuinely time-sensitive

INCLUSIVE CTA GUIDELINES:
- Use action words that don't assume abilities ("explore" vs "see")
- Provide multiple engagement options when possible
- Respect different comfort levels with technology
- Avoid economic pressure or assumptions
- Include accessibility considerations
- Use culturally neutral language

ETHICAL CTA EXAMPLES:
✓ "Explore your options" instead of "Buy now or miss out"
✓ "Learn more about benefits" instead of "Don't wait - limited time"
✓ "See if this works for you" instead of "Everyone's choosing this"
✓ "Take the next step when ready" instead of "Act fast - supplies limited"

FINAL VERIFICATION:
✓ No manipulative pressure tactics
✓ Respects user autonomy and choice
✓ Accessible language and concepts
✓ Culturally appropriate phrasing
✓ Honest about what happens next
✓ Inclusive of diverse audiences
